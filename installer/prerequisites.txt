EARS Bridge - before you start

EARS Bridge lets you use a two-channel miniDSP EARS or EARS Pro headphone jig with
Dirac Live, which only accepts a single calibrated microphone. It applies each ear's
calibration and presents it to Dirac through a virtual audio device as the single
microphone Dirac expects -- following Dirac's sweep so each earcup is measured in turn,
one ear at a time rather than mixing the two.

To actually measure headphones you will also need:

  1. A virtual audio device, so EARS Bridge's output can reach Dirac:
        - VB-CABLE  (https://vb-audio.com/Cable/)

  2. Dirac Live, the room-correction software you are measuring headphones with.

  3. Your EARS / EARS Pro and its per-ear calibration files.

The app itself is self-contained and needs no other runtime. See the project page
for the full step-by-step measurement workflow:

  https://github.com/Elevatormusic/ears-bridge
